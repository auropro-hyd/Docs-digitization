SOP COMPLIANCE RULES

Category: SOP Reference Validation

1. Manufacturing steps reference specific SOP numbers.
2. Referenced SOPs are current revision (not superseded).
3. SOP revision numbers are explicitly stated.
4. All critical operations have an associated SOP reference.

Category: Step Alignment

5. Manufacturing steps follow the sequence defined in the SOP.
6. No steps are skipped without documented justification.
7. Additional steps not in the SOP are documented as deviations.
8. Hold times between steps comply with SOP-defined limits.

Category: Deviation Documentation

9. Deviations from SOP-defined procedures are formally documented.
10. Deviation reports include root cause analysis.
11. Deviation reports are referenced in the BMR.
12. Corrective and preventive actions (CAPA) are documented for deviations.
13. Impact assessment is documented for each deviation.

Category: Parameter Compliance

14. Process parameters match SOP-defined ranges and setpoints.
15. Critical process parameters are monitored at SOP-defined intervals.
16. Out-of-range parameters are documented and investigated.

Category: Material Handling

17. Raw material handling follows SOP-defined procedures.
18. Material storage conditions comply with SOP requirements.
19. Material sampling procedures follow the approved SOP.
20. Waste disposal procedures comply with SOP requirements.
