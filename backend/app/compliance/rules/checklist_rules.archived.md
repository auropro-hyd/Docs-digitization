CHECKLIST REVIEW RULES

Category: Checkbox Completeness

1. All checklist items that require a check mark are completed (checked or marked N/A).
2. No checklist items are left blank without documented justification.
3. Pre-operation checklists are completed before the operation begins.
4. Post-operation checklists are completed after the operation ends.

Category: Signature Verification

5. All required "Done by" signatures are present.
6. All required "Checked by" or "Verified by" signatures are present.
7. All required "Approved by" signatures are present.
8. Supervisor or QA review signatures are present where required.
9. Signatures are accompanied by printed name and date.

Category: Date Completeness

10. All date fields are filled in.
11. Dates follow the defined format (DD/MM/YYYY or as specified).
12. Dates are logically consistent (start date before end date, etc.).
13. No future dates appear for completed activities.

Category: Blank Field Detection

14. No critical data fields are left blank.
15. Fields intentionally left blank are marked "N/A" with initials and date.
16. Calculated fields contain values (not left for later entry).

Category: Attachment Verification

17. All referenced attachments are physically present.
18. Attachments are properly labeled and cross-referenced.
19. Chromatograms, printouts, and labels are securely affixed.
20. No loose or unlabeled attachments are present.

Category: Cross-Contamination Checklists

21. Line clearance checklists are completed before each batch.
22. Equipment-specific cross-contamination checks are documented.
23. Area clearance verification includes previous product removal.

Category: Equipment Operation Checklists

24. Pre-use equipment checks are completed and documented.
25. Equipment parameter settings are recorded per the BMR instruction.
26. Post-use equipment cleaning checklists are completed.
